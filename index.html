The Election Journey 🗳️

1. Notification

The President or Governor issues the notification, kicking off the election season!

2. Nominations

Candidates file their nomination papers and pay a small deposit. Time to step up!

3. Scrutiny

Election officers check if nominations are valid. No rule-breakers allowed! ❌

4. Campaigning

Parties share their manifestos. This lasts for 2 weeks until 48 hours before polling.

5. Polling Day

The big day! Citizens head to booths to cast their votes using EVMs. 👆✨

6. Results

Votes are counted, and the winner is declared. Democracy in action! 🏆

Democracy Flashcards 🎴

Deep Dive into the System 🏛️

Lok Sabha

The House of the People. Members are directly elected by the public every 5 years. Total seats: 543.

Rajya Sabha

The Council of States. Members are elected by State Legislative Assemblies. It is a permanent body.

State Assembly

Vidhan Sabha. Local state-level elections that decide the Chief Minister of your state.
